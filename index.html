About Us

CodeReach is a free tutoring program at Vincent Massey Secondary School. We offer a course through the basics of computer science. It’s taught by students for students.

Next

Goals

CodeReach aims to spark interest as well as introduce coding to kids. We will teach grade 7’s and 8’s the basics of computer science through the use of Python. At the end of our course, students will be able to create their own (End of year project).
